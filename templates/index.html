How It Works

Up and running in under a minute

Four straightforward steps from registration to a fully encrypted, organised password vault.

01
Create your vault
Register with your email address or sign in with Google. Choose a master password — this is the only credential that will ever unlock your entire vault.
02
Organise by notebook
Create named notebooks and add entries with a title, username, site address, and notes. A built-in password generator helps you create strong, unique credentials immediately.

Security Architecture

Zero-knowledge,
by design.

Every cryptographic decision in KeyVora is designed to protect you — even from us. Our servers can never read your passwords.

Your master password never leaves your device
It is used locally to derive your encryption key via PBKDF2-SHA256 with 390,000 iterations. We never see it, store it, or transmit it — not even during the login process.
AES-256 via Fernet — encrypted in your browser
Every password is encrypted using the industry-standard AES-256 symmetric cipher before it leaves your browser. What we store is opaque ciphertext, completely unreadable without your key.
Bcrypt hashing protects your account credentials
Your login password is hashed with Bcrypt — a purpose-built algorithm designed to resist high-speed cracking hardware. Even in the event of a server incident, your account credentials stay safe.
A full database breach exposes nothing readable
Even a full server compromise yields only ciphertext hardened by 390,000 rounds of PBKDF2 key-stretching — computationally infeasible to brute-force without your master password.
Rate-limited auth endpoints block brute-force attacks
Login is capped at 20 requests per minute; registration at 10 per hour; password reset at 5 per hour; vault unlock at 10 per minute. Exceeding any limit returns a clear retry message.
OTP-based password reset with vault key re-wrap
A one-time 6-digit code is sent to your email. Server-side escrow safely re-wraps your vault key under the new master password — your existing passwords are never lost.
Auto-locking vault with a live countdown timer
Your vault key is held only in the server session and wiped automatically after 15 minutes of inactivity. The live timer on your dashboard shows exactly how long remains before auto-lock.
Multi-account sessions — each vault independently locked
Sign in to multiple KeyVora accounts at once. Every account carries its own vault key and lock deadline — switching accounts never mixes or exposes another vault's key.
TOTP two-factor auth with 8 one-time backup codes
Enable TOTP 2FA with any authenticator app. Eight single-use backup codes are generated and hashed with Bcrypt — a used code is permanently deleted, preventing replay attacks.
